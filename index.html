Who We Are

Borealis is a hotel ownership company. We hold and operate a small portfolio of independent and branded hotels, with a focus on properties where careful ownership can make a lasting difference — for guests, for staff, and for the neighborhoods they call home.

We are not a management company for hire, and we are not a short-term investor. Every hotel in our portfolio is one we plan to own for many years.

Our Approach

  • Long-term ownership. We buy hotels we want to hold. Our timelines are measured in decades, not quarters.
  • Operational excellence. We partner closely with on-property teams to run clean, well-maintained, well-loved hotels.
  • Responsible stewardship. Thoughtful capital investment, measured growth, and reinvestment in the properties we own.
  • People first. Hotels are built on the work of their teams. We hire, train, and retain with that in mind.

What We Look For

We focus on select-service, full-service, and boutique hotels in stable markets across the region. We consider both single-asset opportunities and small portfolios, and we are comfortable with repositioning projects where the underlying real estate is sound.

If you own a hotel and are considering a sale, or if you represent a broker or lender with an opportunity that fits our approach, we would welcome a conversation.
