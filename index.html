Boutique Hotels, Rooted in Montana

Borealis Hotels is a husband-and-wife company investing in independent hotels in Montana, with a focus on Missoula and the Flathead Valley.

Get in Touch

A family project

Borealis Hotels was founded by Vince Cusma and Jackie Wells, a husband-and-wife team with careers spent in hospitality. Montana is where Jackie's family has lived and built restaurants for generations — it's the soil this company grew in. For Vince, it's the place he's adopted as home.

Borealis is the project we've always talked about: bringing what we know about operations, service, and ownership together, and putting it to work on boutique hotels in a place we love.

Founders

Vince Cusma, Co-Founder

Vince has spent more than a decade in hotel technology, working with owners and operators around the world on commercial strategy and hotel profitability. He is currently a Senior Product Manager, Enterprise at Lighthouse. Previously, he was VP of Customer Success at Kalibri Labs and began his hotel technology career as the 10th employee at Duetto. Vince holds a B.S. from Cornell University's School of Hotel Administration, with a concentration in Hotel Operations and Technology.

Jackie Wells, Co-Founder

Jackie has managed restaurants in Las Vegas and Washington, D.C., with P&L responsibility between $10–20M annually. She is a trained sommelier and was part of the opening team at a Michelin-starred restaurant, where she led service operations. Her hospitality career began in her family's restaurants in Missoula, Montana. Jackie holds a B.S. in Hotel Management from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, with a minor in Mandarin.

What we're building

We're focused on acquiring boutique, independent hotels in Montana — particularly in Missoula and the Flathead Valley — where thoughtful ownership and operations can make a real difference. We're long-term holders, not short-term investors.

Borealis is new, and we're working toward our first acquisition. If you own a hotel in Montana and are considering a sale, or if you're a broker or operator who shares our approach, we'd welcome a conversation.
